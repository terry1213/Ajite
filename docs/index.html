AJITE

Ajite is a paradigm for music enthusiasts to discover new music and bond with people of similar musical taste. Those who are looking to express their musical interests, those who want to embark on an euphonious adventure,   & those who wish to escape to a musical hideout can find the shelter that they seek with our app.

Create Your Own Ajite

Ajite, a Korean term for 'hideout' or 'shelter', is a room where members who are part of that 'Ajite' can share music with one other. Start your own 'ajite' to start sharing music with friends that you wish to connect with.

Create your 'Ajite' by navigating to the Create Ajite view. Choose a name and add your friends to your ajite to open up a new room. You will receive notificiations every time you are invited to a new Ajite. You can also add new friends to an existing ajite.

Inside the Ajite

An Ajite is split into two sections.
The top screen functions as a TV screen that encases the video you wish to play. The bottom section stores all the shared songs that you and your members have shared into your Ajite in chronological order (along with the person who shared the song in the Ajite.)




Press the share button on the upper-right hand corner to open up a pop-up that you can use to search through Youtube for music that you wish to share.





Our application uses Youtube API to fetch and play Youtube Videos. This allows users to share much more than just official releases from artists. Users can also share cover music, re-interpretations of songs that were played in concerts, and original content exclusive to only Youtube from youtubers worldwide. Press the song you wish to listen to and the video will pop up on the screen above. Users of our app can enjoy automatic streaming of songs shared in a playlist or an ajite.

Share & Discover

Assemble together a playlist of your own! There are many ways you can add to your personal playlists.

Our app features a service where you can add songs into your personal playlists.
There are many ways to accomplish this. 1 You can directly add music to your profile using the Plus tab on the top-right hand corner of the inside of your playlist view. 2 You can also re-add songs from one playlist to another. 3  Another way is to add songs from the "Shared-Songs" section in your Ajites to your own playlists. Press the 'plus' button to add music that you or your friends shared to your playlists. 4  Discover music from your friend's public playlists.
